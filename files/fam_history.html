Family History

I was blessed at birth with many talented ancestors who accomplished remarkable feats and have inspired me to work at becoming the best possible version of myself.

The World's First Flight

Wilbur & Orville Wright: Keith's Great-Great Granduncles

On December 17th, 1903 my ancestors successfully flew the first sustained, controlled, powered heavier-than-air flight in a manned aircraft. 100 years later as the United States was celebrating the centennial of their achievement, I had the honor of travelling the country and seeing the significance of the industry they had started. This gave me the opportunity to meet aerospace pioneers like Chuck Yeager and Neil Armstrong when at a very young age, and I credit these meetings with setting me down the path to becoming an engineer.
